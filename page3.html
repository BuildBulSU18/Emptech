Have you ever experienced going home but never finding any home to go inside in? Have you ever felt so helplessly tired in all your life? Well, imagine that, but in a school setting. It is only natural that after every lecture or laboratory session, students want to just sit down and rest their minds. The fact of the matter is, not all of them experience this luxury. We welcome you to the reality that BulSU students experience everyday. 

No chairs to sit in, no tables to write on, no boards to discuss with, no fans to cool with and no comfort to be found. Without a room, we are forced outside. That is what we experience. At times, there is even no room outside. Standing around with nothing to do or where to go is awful. Productivity is lost where productivity should be attained. It is probably the worst thing to experience inside school. Quite ironic, as the school is often called the "second home". Homes are supposed to be places where we can lie down, find comfort and not worry about anything. What happened?

We say all this, but the institution is not all at fault. In the end, the demand for high quality education within the institution is at its peaks. The university only wanted to provide this oppurtunity to multiple aspiring students and even though there are no rooms to rest in, the university still provides said quality education. In a way, it's a give n' take relationship. It's all a matter of willing sacrifice as the results of this struggle will pay off in the future.  

Why this topic? Again, the issue is very much real and observable. Ideally, no student should experience that. They deserve better. We deserve better. For this to happen, change must happen. Awareness on this topic is essential. People need to know what they need to solve first before considering any procedure for change. This is the main reason why we want to create the website. We want change... and awareness is the first step towards change.
